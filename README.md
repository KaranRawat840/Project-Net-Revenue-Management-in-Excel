# Project-Net-Revenue-Management-in-Excel
The world of Fast-Moving Consumer Goods (FMCG) is a dynamic world that plays a significant role in our daily lives.  I always wondered how well-known brands like Coca-Cola, Nivea, and Lay's are being managed? This case study helped me to get a better understanding of this industry.

I started by analyzing a big dataset and by performing your own market analysis. I calculated market share to creating clear visuals to reveal actionable insights. Next, I used the principles of Net Revenue Management (NRM), which will help me to identify new growth opportunities for the company.

I used a variety of Excel tools and functions to calculate the true potential of these growth buckets. I will not only master everything which relates to PivotTables, VLOOKUP, and forecasting but also have a better understanding of what is happening behind the scenes of my favorite FMCG brands.
